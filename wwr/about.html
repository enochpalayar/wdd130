History

Rapids Rafting Company was indeed born in the early 1990s as an offspring of the adventurous outdoor enthusiasts showing interest in doing whitewater rafting. Not far from some of the country's best rivers, the business began quickly in terms of gaining popularity since it was very keen on extending exhilarating and safe rafting adventures. It began by offering rafting tours for beginners but soon took this into more challenging rapids. It rapidly became the most thrilling experience of rapids - with a high safety standard and friendlier staff - attracting adventure-seeking tourists from all over the world.
