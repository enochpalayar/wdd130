Philippines

Philippines is an archipelago comprising of over 7,000 islands with different cultures and traditions. So infused with pretty beaches, mountains and cities and with the people always depicted as warm and hospitality, Philippines hold a tight bond of family closeness comes a deep sense of community. The clashes of Eastern and Western influences in the Philippines show in its festivals, cuisines and art forms that will resonate their past. Yet despite all of this, the Filipino spirit remains strong and hopeful which makes the nation a vibrant albeit warm one.
